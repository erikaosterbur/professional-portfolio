About Me

Hello! My name is Erika and I am a former educator turned web developer. I taught in elementary school for nine years and am seeking a career change into the world of web development. I enjoy designing beautiful websites that have intuitive UIs. In college, I studied Spanish and have lived in Spanish-speaking countries for extended amounts of time. I then taught in a bilingual classroom in Minneapolis Public Schools, providing high-quality education to native Spanish-speaking students. In my free time, I enjoy hiking with my family, camping, knitting, and traveling. My partner and I love to travel all over the world.
